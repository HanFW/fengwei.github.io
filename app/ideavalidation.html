Ideation & Lean startup methodology
INTRODUCTION

This is a course that I took under an entrepreneurship program in my university. During this course, we formed a team of three and learnt the lean startup methodology together through brainstorming ideas for a digital product, building business model canvas, doing interviews with users, and iterating. We conducted many interviews and surveys in our school and shopping malls to validate our ideas. It was not a easy process to go through, but we feel realy grateful for all the people who took their time to finish our surveys and even help to spread them online.

Product Overview

At first we put our focus on solving language and logistic problems for the customers, so we decided to create an online shopping platform, where all suppliers are from Taobao but all the services are provided in English. At the same time, we wanted to make it easier and faster for customers to ship their products.

Lean startup methodology

We first conducted 16 interviews in order to get more information and insights through open-ended questions. After that, we designed survey questions based on the interviews and conducted 2 rounds of surveys, with 100 respondants in the first round and 62 in the second round. The surveys were conducted with random people in shopping malls and in the university.

PIVOT

After two rounds of validation and the interim presentation, a few of our initial value propositions are invalidated, so we decided to pivot. Instead of addressing the language barrier and delivery issues, we changed our focus to the shopping experience and the quality of the products, based on the feedbacks and insights from previous interviews and surveys. We then did more validation for our new value propositions, and designed a very simple minimal viable product for the new idea.
