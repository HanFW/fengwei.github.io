Merlion Banking System

Enterprise Application Design & Implementation
INTRODUCTION

This is the capstone project of my major - Information Systems, and is definitely the project that I am most proud of. Being the Tech Lead, I successfully led a team of 6 to design and develop a whole online banking system in three months. Although the overall programming skill of our team was below the average when we started, we worked almost 12 hours together every day to catch up and transcend. We were extremely excited that we finally achieved a band-one grade.


The system consists of three parts: an online banking system used by customers, an internal system to facilitate employees in the bank, and an external system used to simulate all the transactions with external parties. The external system is hosted separately from the other two.
